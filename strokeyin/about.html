KSISA strives to be the primary resource center for those throughout Kentucky and Southern Indiana affected by stroke. We provide speakers and information on prevention and recovery to the general public and our survivors and families through local community events, health fairs, stroke clubs, support groups, etc
Four years after we began operations, we recruited the one and only staff member KSISA has ever had the honor to employee. All of the educational events, awareness efforts, and fundraisers fall on the shoulders of our Executive Director. She is the one pulling the strings that keep our mission moving forward. Her endless efforts to garner support from the community does not go unnoticed. Without all of her hard work KSISA would not be what it has become in the 17 years (as of 2020) she has been with us. Her loyalty and enthusiasm is what brings people in to assist in our mission. She is a pleasure to work with as evidenced by our countless volunteers and dedicated Board Members. This organization has made an impact on so many lives in the Kentuckiana area and much of that has been from her unyielding desire to make a difference in our community.
The funds donated to and raised by the Kentuckiana Stroke Association stay local. They go to pushing our mission forward here in the Kentucky and Southern IN area. Donate with confidence that your money is going toward a good cause right in your own back yard.*

JOIN US TO LEARN HOW YOU CAN GET INVOLVED

Learn more about KSISA and how you can be involved by giving us a call, email or join us for one of our Board meetings that are held the 2nd Wednesday of each month at 4 p.m. at Baptist Health in the education bldg. and are open to everyone from February to November.
